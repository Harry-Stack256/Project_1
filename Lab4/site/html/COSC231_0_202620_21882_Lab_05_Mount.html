Sample Maximum: xmax= xn xmax= 9
Sample Sum: (i-1:n)Σxi Σ = 5
Sample Mean: x̅= (Σxi) /n x̅ = 5
Sample Median: 1° (n%2==1): x̅ = x(n-1)/2
2° (n%2==0) x̅ = (xn/2+xn/2+1)/2		 x̅ = 5
Sample Variance: s2 = (Σ(xi-x)2)/n-1 s2 = 10
